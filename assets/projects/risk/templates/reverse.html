Reverse Evacuation

ACTION STEPS (Teacher/Staff)

  1. Blow three loud and sharp whistle blasts to get students attention (Stop, Look, and Listen).
  2. Utilize hand signals for direction, urgency, and safety commands.
  3. Ensure care for those students with special needs.
  4. Initiate Soft Lockdown or Shelter-in-Place procedures as directed, once in the building.
