Hard Lockdown

ACTION STEPS (Teacher/Staff)

If outdoors:

  1. Independently Evacuate

If indoors:

  1. Move students to a secure location behind a lockable door or Independently Evacuate.
  2. Lock the door(s).
  3. Lights Off.
  4. Cover Windows.
  5. Students should Duck and Cover.
  6. Take attendance.
