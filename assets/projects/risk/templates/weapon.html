ACTION STEPS (Teacher/Staff)

If the incident occurs near you:

  1. Hard Lockdown or Independent Evacuation should be determined purely on the use of judgement and/or instinct given the situation.
  2. Do not attempt to disarm the perpetrator as this may escalate the situation.
  3. No sudden or unexpected movement, remain calm.
  4. Alert Site Administrator of the situation by the fastest and safest available means.
