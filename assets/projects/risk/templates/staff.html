Staff & Student Accounting

ACTION STEPS (Teacher/Staff)

Evacuation

  1. Display teacher identification sign at the fire drill location, Staff-Student Staging Area, and/or Reunification Site.
  2. Attach Green Card (all present) or Red Card (assistance needed).
  3. Label each student, if continuing on to Family Reunification site.
  4. If no students, report to Staff Coordinator for appropriate assignment.
